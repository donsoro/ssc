UNDP will facilitate adaptation of Climate Change (CC) knowledge, lessons and experiences from the region are used to inform national and regional policies and CC interventions at community level. Actions: Disseminate lessons learnt in regional forums such as Southern Africa Development Community (SADC) and Common Market for Eastern and Southern Africa (COMESA) as a way of influencing regional policy and financing; support capacity of Mozambican negotiators to actively convene such information; Link to international Adaptation Learning Mechanism (ALM) of UNDP to ensure multi-directional flow of learning experiences on CCA.
