UNDP's role in this programme is to assist the participating Pacific countries in the development and dissemination of best practice for building national and local capacity for IWRM; Facilitating transfer of IWRM regional practice from one PIC to another; Identification and documentation of existing small island IWRM practice at different scales;
and Facilitation of regional coordination on IWRM issues (Knowledge, partnership and capacity)
