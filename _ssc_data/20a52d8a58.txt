In partnership with UNDP Pacific Centre, (1) supports to build linkages between the regions' SIDS in DRR including gender analysis; capacity building; and sharing experiences in addressing DRR and climate change adaptation; (2) supports and coordinates exchange missions in both regions; (3) facilitates training of Pacific weather observers at the Caribbean Institute for Meteorology and Hydrology; and participation in regional fora addressing climate change adaptation and DRR. A guide was published for integrating gender in Disaster Management in SIDS utilizing expertise from the respective Caribbean and Pacific SIDS. (this is a cross-regional initiative)
