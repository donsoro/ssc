UNDP provided capacity building and supported the knowledge transfer. Two training programs were organized with experts from Peru and Colombia on Indigenous People's rights to consultation. As a result, recommendations were formulated for the elaboration of the draft law on the right to consultation. An improved draft law proposal was formulated.
