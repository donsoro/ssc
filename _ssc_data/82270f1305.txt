UNDP supports knowledge exchange among Haiti, Cuba and Brazil. UNDP Haiti experience and know how in rebuilding the country will serve as an important component. Missions to monitor the progress and exchange experience are planned. UNDP is also responsible for the development of planning activities, monitoring and assistance in the procurement of goods and services.
