UNDP facilitated knowledge exchange among Cuba, Bolivia and Uruguay on biomass energy. Uruguay received a Cuban mission and shared their experience of PROBIO Project and biomass sector. In accordance with the south-south cooperation agreement between Uruguay and Bolivia, the Ministry of Planning of Uruguay hosted an study visit for the Bolivian delegation and presented their experience.
