UNDP supported regional knowledge exchange on best practice on Local Economic Development experiences,  and lessons learned on support to national strategies and policies. As a result, a joint publication was produced on successful experiences of DEL in Latin America; best practices were presented in the II World Forum on LED in Brazil, in October 2013.
