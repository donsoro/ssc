On 18th Dec 2014, the Government of Malawi published the Public Service Reform Commission Report entitled “Looking into the Future - Making Malawi Work by Transforming Malawi’s Public Service”. The report reflects some of the resolutions noted during the experience sharing on making the public service efficient and support economic growth by empowering commercial parastatal organization to be given more autonomy to operate like companies and enter into joint ventures.
