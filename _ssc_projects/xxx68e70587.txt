This knowledge exchange visit between Togo and Cote D’Ivoire has strengthened the capacities of national stakeholders from Togo who are in charge of the urbanization and development Plan of Lome (SDAU) in the utilization of methodologies, tools and good practices. This has allowed Togo to deepen its baseline diagnosis and to launch the development of the SDAU process in good conditions.
