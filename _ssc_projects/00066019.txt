UNDP facilitates knowledge sharing and plays the role of a regional coordinating unit. UNDP/GEF shall provide a format and assist the project team in categorizing, documenting and reporting on lessons learned. These lessons will be shared widely throughout DEDE – and MOEN – to help develop and initiate ongoing projects and new initiatives. Such mechanism for sharing will include newsletter, websites, and technical and general publications.
