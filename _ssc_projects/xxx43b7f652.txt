project_title: The Social Cohesion and Reconciliation Index (SCORE)
scale: country-country
project_objective: The SCORE is designed to assess social cohesion and reconciliation. Its findings can be used to yield both descriptive and predictive outputs, providing practitioners and researchers the necessary insights for designing better targeted and efficient peace building interventions. The SCORE has been run in Cyprus in 2013 and 2014, as well as in Bosnia in 2014. The findings will be presented in an online platform in 2015 and in various events addressed to the local and international public.
