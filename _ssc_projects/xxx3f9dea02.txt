 Support to the Environment, Sustainable development and Social Affairs commission of the Tanzanian parliament to put in place a sub-commission in charge of facilitating south-south exchanges around best practices on renewable and clean energies. Facilitation of exchanges around improved stoves during a conference between a NGO that has set up a network of producers and the Tanzanian sub-commission. Three sensitization campaigns have been supported by efficiency tests of the stoves.
