South South strategy of the Project- the Ecosystem Based Adaptation global programme will facilitate sharing of best practices and lessons learned within and outside the 3 participating developing countries of Nepal, Peru and Uganda. It will serve as a platform for developing and strengthening the adaptation tools and methodologies at the global level, using practical field experiences.
