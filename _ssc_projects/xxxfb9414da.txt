UNDP promoted the exchange agreement between the Dominican Local Economic Development Agencies network and Cuba's National Agricultural Health Center (CENSA) with the aim of supporting a territorial economic development public policy in Dominican Republic. Transfer of the experience was organized on development and implementation of an integrated  technical support programme for  the entire dairy chain that included both entities and producers, collectors, local processors as well as national regulatory entities (Dairy National Council, CONALECHE)
