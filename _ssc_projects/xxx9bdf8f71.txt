UNDP facilitated knowledge exchange between Togo and South Africa on national reconciliation. The missions have allowed for a better understanding and a common vision by national stakeholders around the national reconciliation process. It is planned to further extend the exchange and valorize the experience of both pays to adopt them to the context of Madagascar.
