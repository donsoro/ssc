301 partnerships, valued at over USD 1.9 billion [UN], were registered during the conference, to address a broad range of challenges facing SIDS, between governments, businesses and CSOs from all over the world. Over one-third of the partnerships registered were announced during the conference, including new partnerships worth potential USD625 million, while existing partnerships made new commitments amounting to USD $1.28 billion.
