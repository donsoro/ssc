 UNDP has facilitated a study tour between Ivory Coast and Rwanda allowing National Security Stakeholders (Police, Gendarms, National Security Council, Ministry of Internal Affairs and National Assembly) to study good practices. The initiative has taken place at the end of 2014 (December) and has so far allowed for initial meetings for the development of performance indicators of the National Police. Participants were satisfied by the tour.
