This mission of two Nigerian professionals to Rwanda has allowed for the Niger High Commission for State Modernization to learn about the tools and procedures Rwanda uses in the simplification process of public service procedures. They were also able to understand strategic areas that constitute the baseline for the development of a modern public administration. Further findings from the mission to improve results in Niger continue to be explored as the mission has only taken place at the end of November (report is available)
