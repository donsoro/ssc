UNDP will provide technical assitance for the creation of IC4HD. The Centre will- (1) establish cross country/regional mechanisms whereby practitioners can collaborate and support the adaptation of good practices to local context; (2) provide content and training support on human development to government officials, community leaders, academia, media and parliamentarians through existing institutions; (3) provide a multistakeholder platform for south south cooperation on HD action to foster mutual learning.
