In November 2014 a follow-up consultation was held in Kinshasa to agree on the following key action points to implement the AAP - Strengthen existing SS&TC partnerships; Scale-up SSC regional development projects; Mainstream SSC&TC in regional political and economic institutions; Support the removal of barriers to free movement of people, capital, services and goods for intra-Africa SSC; Development of national and regional SS&TC policies for economic development; and Develop monitoring standards for SS&TC.
