The project has facilitated South-South Partnerships between NGOs at communal level as well as social innovative technology transferts between Brazil and Cabo Verde.  In the area of community and youth interventions, 4 national NGOs have been trained through the transfer of social technologies (SSC), most particularly through 4 missions of 9 experts from the Brazilian NGO “Afro Reggae” during one months and an inclusive training on project development for approximately 60 participants.
