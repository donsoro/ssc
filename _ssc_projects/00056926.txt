UNDP facilitated knowledge ecxhange between Brazil and Cuba on the development of family farming, management of water resources, livestock management, soil resource management and food production. As a result, scientific cooperation between Cuba and Brazil was established, Cuban experts were trained in sustainable land management, Cuban producers were trained in production techniques and food security.
