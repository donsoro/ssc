High level technical advice for drafting the country's new National Poverty Profile was provided to the Ministry of Economics and Planning by the National Statistics Institute of Mexico, in which UNDP facilitated. As a result of the knowlege exhcnage and capacity building provided by the Mexican counterparts, the Equatorial Guinea was able to prepare all of the needed  technical documentation, including a very detailed road map for undertaking its new poverty strategy.
