The mission and knowledge exchange between Kenya and Comoros has allowed for a diagnosis of the telecommunication system and the Meteorologic Observation network, to suggest solutions for meteorologic telecommunication and to redefine needs for collection and data transmitting equipments (this includes links with other institutions and solutions to improve data collection) as well as to suggest solutions in the acquisition of satellite data, meteorologic and climate products.
