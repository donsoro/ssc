This Study tour of Nigerian professionals to Senegal has allowed for a better understanding of the methodology to identify and choose intervention sites and collect baseline data for the index based agriculture insurance project. Thanks to lessons learnt, Nigeria has improved the preparation of its pilot project for the index-based climate insurance against drought. I has adopted a similar project implementation site identification methodology and better collection methods.
