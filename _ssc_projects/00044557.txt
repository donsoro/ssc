UNDP facilitates partnership and dialogue among territories, promotes South South and triangular cooperation for introducing mechanisms of aid efficiency. As a result, international and decentralized cooperation, South South Cooperation will be advanced through- identification of national and international partners; organizing exchange visits and sharing of experience; signing of partnership agreements with international cooperation structures.
