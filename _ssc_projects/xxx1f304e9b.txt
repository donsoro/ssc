UNDP facilitated the knowledge sharing around the Nigerian experience of the Disaster Impact database. The database has been installed in the Directorate for Civil Protection in Mali and a training workshop has introduced the national structures to its usage. Data collection was launched with the support of the Mali office. Furthermore, a Nigerian specialist for Crisis and Disaster prevention has also extended its knowledge, most particularly on the usage of the database for the development of the country profile.
