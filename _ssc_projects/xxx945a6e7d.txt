UNDP supported a mission between the Governments of Comoros and Madagascar leading to the development of a roadmap for the establishment of a partnership between national and international stakeholders involved into the Conservation Programme of “Eulemur Mongoz”. The mission has also allowed to an improved definition of their respective roles, a better understanding by national stakeholders of the challenges linked to the conservation of Eulemur Mongoz, including the definition of technical collaboration modalities for the Expert Group for research on apes and local actors.
