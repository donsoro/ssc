PACC -  construction of a climate proof harbor in Cook Islands; completion of 8.5km climate resilient roads in Micronesia & Vanuatu; installation of water tanks and solar purifiers in Tonga, Nauru, Niue through; research and promotion of climate resilient crops (taro, cassava, potatoes) in Fiji.
PIGGAREP - Installation of 10 solar water pump systems in Tonga, and sharing their knowledge with Palau; change from fossil fuel to solar the main power source of Palmerston in Cook Islands.
