UNDP facilitated the exchange of experience between Ecuador, Brazil and Uruguay on Public Investment Systems. Several missions and trainings were organized to exchange experience, e.g. mission of Uruguay's Planning and Budget Office (OPP) to Brazil's Ministry of Planning. A joint Work Plan was agreed by National Secretariat of Planning and Development of Ecuador (SENPLADES) and OPP Uruguay.
