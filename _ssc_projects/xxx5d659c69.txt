  PNUD has facilited the deployment of 3N experts from Niger in Tchad. This mission of Nigerian experts in Chad has allowed to accelerate the process “Global Alliance for the Resilience Initiative in Sahel”, most particularly in defining priorities and the different pillars of the initiatives. As a next step, it is planned to  strengthen the cooperation between the two countries in the area of resilience and to conduct a study visit from Chad to Niger to learn about the Niger experience.
