Adoption of the International Convention on integrated water resources management of Lake Kivu by the Ministerial Council of the lake Kivu and Ruzizi River on October 4th 2014 in Kinshasa. This allows to establish common appropriate measures and regulations for anthropogenic and endogenic activities at Lake Kivu between DRC and Rwanda.
